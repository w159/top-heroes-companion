# Eggy

**Faction**: League
**Rarity**: Legendary
**Role**: Critical Buffs

## Overview

Eggy is the **Best-in-Slot** pet for League teams. League heroes (like Paragon) rely heavily on Critical Hits and Shields, and Eggy supercharges this playstyle.

## Skills

### Active: Egg Bomb

Deals AoE damage.

### Passive: Critical Surge

Provides a massive boost to **Critical Rate**.

- **Scaling**: Unlike flat attack buffs, Critical Rate scales exceptionally well into the late game.

## Stat Bonuses

Provides **All League Hero Attack** and **All League Hero HP**.

## Strategy

- **Meta**: League teams fall behind if they don't crit. Eggy ensures consistency.
- **Pairing**: Mandatory for **Paragon** active squads.
