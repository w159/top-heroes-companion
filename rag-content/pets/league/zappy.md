# Zappy

**Faction**: League
**Rarity**: Legendary
**Role**: AoE Damage

## Overview

Zappy is the AoE-focused alternative for League.

## Skills

### Active: Chain Lightning

Bounces between enemies dealing damage.

### Passive: Static Field

Increases damage against shielded enemies (Synergizes with League's shield mechanics).

## Strategy

- **Usage**: Good for PvE content where AoE is king (like Adventure Stages), but **Eggy** is preferred for PvP and Bosses due to Critical Rate consistency.
