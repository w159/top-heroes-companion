## Tier List Notes

### Focus on S-Tier First

- Build your S-tier heroes before investing in lower tiers
- S-tier heroes remain relevant throughout the game

### A-Tier Are Still Great

- Don't ignore A-tier, especially healers and tanks
- Some A-tier are essential for specific roles

### Faction Priority Matters

- Tier rankings within your chosen faction matter more
- Don't invest in S-tier from a faction you won't main

### Meta Changes

- Tier list updated for Season 3
- New heroes may shift rankings
- Check Discord for latest meta discussions
