# Petalis

## Overview

- **Faction**: Nature
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Hybrid Support (AoE, Damage Dealer, Supporter)
- **Position**: Back / Mid

## Skills

### Sacred Lotus (Active)

**Cooldown:** 8s

Raises petals to protect 1 allied hero (front row first), granting a shield equal to 280% of Petalis' Attack. The shield absorbs 80% of incoming damage and lasts 6 seconds. While active, it prevents Silence and Knockback. If broken early, it deals 192% of Petalis' Attack as damage to nearby enemies.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Shield value increases by 50%, protected hero count +1, and the holder gains 12% Damage Reduction while active.
- ★★ Shield value increases by 50%, protected hero count +1.
- ★★★ Shield value increases by 50%, protected hero count +1, and the holder gains 12% Damage Reduction while active.

### Charm Flower (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 24s

Launches a heart made of flowers that deals 410% of Attack as damage to units along its path. The heart explodes at the center of the enemy team, dealing 390% of Attack as damage to all enemies in the area and applying Charm for 1.5 seconds. Deals 40% reduced damage to shields.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Path and explosion damage coefficients increased by 45%. Charmed targets take 30% increased skill damage for 3 seconds.
- ★★ Path and explosion damage coefficients increased by 45%.
- ★★★ Path and explosion damage coefficients increased by 45%. For each enemy unit charmed, increases the damage of the next Charm Flower.

### Flourish (Passive)

When allied units (excluding Petalis) cast skills, Petalis collects 1 stack of Sunlight. Each stack increases the shield value of the next Sacred Lotus by 1.5%. Can stack up to 8 times; all stacks are consumed on cast.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Maximum Sunlight stacks +2.
- ★★ Sunlight stacks gained per trigger +1.
- ★★★ Maximum Sunlight stacks +2.

### Twin Lotus (Active)

**Cooldown:** 48s

When an allied hero (excluding Petalis) falls below 20% HP, Petalis consumes 20% of her HP to apply a shield equal to 38% of her max HP. The shield absorbs 80% of damage and lasts 6 seconds. If there are 6 Nature heroes in the team, the shield value is doubled.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Shield value increases by 15%, and Petalis' HP consumption is reduced to 10%.
- ★★ Shield absorption increases by 15%.
- ★★★ Shield value increases by 15%, and cooldown is reduced by 6 seconds.

### Sakura Rain (Active)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Summons Sakura Rain for 6 seconds. Allied units inside gain 26% Damage Increase. Enemy units inside lose 30% Attack Speed and 15% Attack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Damage bonus increases by 2%, and allied units in range gain 15% Critical Damage.
- ★★ Damage bonus increases by 2%.
- ★★★ Damage bonus increases by 2%. Range is increased and duration extended by 2 seconds.

### Fox Spirit Secrets (Passive)

For each Nature hero in the team:

- **Attacking:** All allies gain 1.5% Damage Reduction (max 9%)
- **Defending:** All allies gain 1.5% Damage Increase (max 9%)

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Glory of the Knight** (Primary)

- Stats: +10% Attack, +20% HP, +4% Skill Amp
- Shield value scales with ATK

**Alternative:** Fury of Blood

- Used for increased survivability or synergy with Tidecaller

**Stats Priority:**

- Attack (shields scale)
- Skill Amplify
- HP

## Team Synergies

- **Nature Core**: Full Nature team doubles Twin Lotus shield
- **Best With**: High damage dealers who benefit from Sakura Rain
- **Replacement Note**: Superior, high-damage support replacement for Sage in current meta

## Tips

- Sacred Lotus prevents Silence and Knockback - incredible utility
- Twin Lotus doubles with 6 Nature heroes - build around this
- Charm Flower at ★★★ grows stronger with each charmed enemy
- Sakura Rain provides massive team damage buff AND enemy debuff
- One of the best Nature supports in the game
