# Witch

## Overview

- **Faction**: Horde
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Healer / Control / Support
- **Specialties**: Caster/Support, Sustained
- **Position**: Back / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Codex of Shadows
- **Unlock**: Purchase or Event

## Skills

### Dark Healing (Active)

**Cooldown:** 4s

Heals an ally's HP equal to 460% of Attack, bouncing 2 times. Each bounce reduces healing by 10%.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ HP recovery additionally increases by 87.5%, and increases the target's Damage Reduction by 25% for 3 seconds.
- ★★ HP recovery increases by 87.5%.
- ★★★ Healing boost by 87.5%, and Dark Healing gains +2 additional bounces.

### Dread Golem (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 22s

Summons a Dread Golem, making enemies in its fear range uncontrollable and move backward for 2 seconds. The golem lasts 3 seconds, dealing damage equal to 14% of its max HP per second to all nearby enemies, up to 140% of its Attack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Max HP ratio boost increases by 3%, cap coefficient increases by 30%, and golem duration is extended by 2 seconds.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 3%.
- ★★★ Max HP ratio boost increases by 3%, cap coefficient increases by 30%. Each golem attack increases the target's damage taken by 4% for 3.5 seconds.

### Curse Fire (Active)

_Triggers after Dark Healing_

After using Dark Healing, apply 2 Curse Fires to random allies, reducing damage taken by 20% and healing HP equal to 15% of Attack per second for 6 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Damage Reduction bonus increases by an additional 2.5%, and also heals the target's HP equal to 100% of Witch's Attack per second.
- ★★ Damage Reduction bonus increases by an extra 2.5%.
- ★★★ Damage Reduction bonus further increases by 2.5%, and Curse Fire count is increased by 2.

### Dark Magic Boost (Active)

**Cooldown:** 14s

Casts dark magic on 3 allies, giving them a 15% normal attack boost for 6 seconds and a barrier that blocks 1 stun. The barrier lasts 16 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Normal attack boost increases by 2.5%. Dark magic also grants 60% Attack Speed and 40% HP Drain.
- ★★ Normal attack damage bonus increases by 2.5%.
- ★★★ Normal attack boost increases by 2.5%, and Dark Magic Boost applies to all heroes.

### Dark Magic Shield (Passive)

Max damage received per hit is capped at 22.5% of max HP, and skill damage taken is reduced by 2.5%.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage reduction increases by 2.5%.
- ★★ Reduces the maximum damage taken per hit by an additional 2.5% and increases skill damage reduction by 2.5%. At the beginning of battle, permanently reduces healing received by all enemy heroes by 50%.
- ★★★ Further improves damage cap and skill damage reduction.

### Guardian Messenger (Passive)

Increases soldier HP by 12% for every 1000 soldiers deployed. Only one Guardian Messenger works per team; effects do not stack.

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Glory of the Knight** (Default)

- Stats: +10% ATK, +20% HP, +4% Skill Amp
- Notes: ATK-scaling weapon; also needs HP for survivability

**Alternative:** Fury of Blood

- Recommended for specific sustain setups

**Stats Priority:**

- Attack (healing scales with ATK)
- HP
- Skill Amplify
- Damage Reduction

## Upgrade Priority

1. **Dark Healing to ★★★** - Core healing ability with extra bounces
2. **Dark Magic Boost to ★★★** - Team-wide buffs are incredibly powerful
3. **Dark Magic Shield to ★★** - Healing reduction on enemies is game-changing
4. **Dread Golem to ★★★** - Damage amplification debuff

## Team Synergies

- **Best With**: High Attack DPS heroes who benefit from Dark Magic Boost
- **Queue Example**: Witch, Wanderer, Storm Maiden, Warlock, Barbarian

## Tips

- ★★ Dark Magic Shield permanently reduces enemy healing by 50% - counters heal-heavy teams
- Dark Magic Boost at ★★★ applies to ALL heroes - massive team buff
- Curse Fire provides sustained healing over time in addition to Dark Healing
- Dread Golem's fear disrupts enemy formations and positioning
- One of the best Horde healers with strong offensive utility
