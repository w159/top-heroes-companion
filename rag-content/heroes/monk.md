# Monk

## Overview

- **Faction**: Nature
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Tank / Shield (Primary: Guardian)
- **Position**: Front / Mid / Back
- **Unlock**: Purchase or Event

## Skills

### Mountain Strike (Active)

**Cooldown:** 6s

Delivers a powerful punch, dealing 135% of Attack as damage in a rectangular area in front. Increases Monk's max HP by 15% and grants 25% damage immunity for 10 seconds (stacks up to 2 times).

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Increases skill damage by 15% and reduces minimum cooldown by 1 second.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 15%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 15%, and damage reduction increases by 10%.

### Ultimate Zen Power (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Enters a defensive counter state with a bamboo staff. Gains 20 Dodge and 35% damage reduction for 6 seconds. Blocks incoming attacks and counterattacks for 100% of Attack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Effect lasts 1 second longer. Deals 100% Attack damage per second to the nearest enemy while active.
- ★★ Increases duration by 1 second.
- ★★★ Increases duration by 1 second.

### Thundering Kick (Passive)

50% chance to perform a follow-up kick when using Mountain Strike, dealing 130% of Attack as damage in a cone and stunning targets for 1.5 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage increases by 20%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 20%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 20%, and trigger chance increases by 20%.

### Fiery Breath (Active)

**Cooldown:** 10s

Spits fire while remaining immune to control effects. Deals damage equal to 7% of Monk's max HP per second to enemies in a cone for 3 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ HP-based damage increases by 1%, and Monk restores HP equal to the damage dealt.
- ★★ HP-based damage increases by 1%.
- ★★★ HP-based damage increases by 1%.

### Merciful Victor (Passive)

When any unit (including summons) dies, all units gain a shield equal to 7% of the target's max HP (absorbs 50% damage) for 8 seconds. Shields stack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Shield absorption increases by 1%. Each Legendary Nature hero in the team reduces damage taken from Horde heroes by 3% (up to 18%).
- ★★ Shield absorption increases by 1%.
- ★★★ Shield absorption increases by 1%. Allies also gain +15% max HP for 8 seconds when triggered.

### Guardian Messenger (Passive)

Increases soldier HP by 12% for every 1000 soldiers deployed.

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Fury of Blood** (Primary)

- Stats: +40% Gear HP, +3% Damage Reduction
- Tank/Shield focus

**Alternative:** Glory of the Knight

**Stats Priority:**

- HP (scales Fiery Breath and shields)
- Damage Reduction
- Block Chance

## Team Synergies

- **Bonds**: Petalis & Tidecaller (15% Damage Reduction)
- **Nature Core**: Merciful Victor reduces Horde damage with Nature allies

## Tips

- Mountain Strike stacks make Monk incredibly tanky over time
- Ultimate Zen Power is a powerful counter-state - 35% DR + dodge + counter
- Thundering Kick at ★★★ has 70% trigger chance - frequent stuns
- Fiery Breath at ★ heals Monk for damage dealt - sustain
- Merciful Victor synergizes with summoner teams - shields on death
- One of the best Nature tanks with excellent sustain
