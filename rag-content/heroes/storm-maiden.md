# Storm Maiden

## Overview

- **Faction**: Horde
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Damage Dealer
- **Specialties**: Multi-Target, Sustained Damage
- **Position**: Back / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Stormbringer
- **Unlock**: Purchase or Event

## Skills

### Whirlwind Feathers (Active)

**Cooldown:** 8s

Unleashes 2 feather projectiles per second to randomly attack enemies within range, dealing 157.5% attack damage for 3 seconds. This skill can be used while performing basic attacks.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Increases skill damage by 17.5%, and deals bonus damage equal to 2% of the target's current HP each time (up to a maximum of 100% of Storm Maiden's Attack).
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 17.5%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 17.5%, and increases attack speed by 100% upon activation.

### Lightning Storm (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 22s

Releases a lightning-infused tornado that moves slowly, dealing damage equal to 355% of Attack to enemies in its path and knocking them into the air.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage increases by 55%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 55%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 55%. After using this skill, Storm Maiden's normal attack becomes Chain Lightning, jumping to up to 3 targets and lasting 6 seconds.

### Wings of Freedom (Passive)

Grants 3% dodge rate to this hero, and additionally grants the same dodge bonus to the entire queue when deployed.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Increases the dodge effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★ Increases the dodge effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★★ Increases the dodge bonus by 1%, and grants Storm Maiden 10% increased damage (self-only).

### Tornado (Active)

**Cooldown:** 12s

Channels a whirlwind for 2 seconds, becoming immune to bullet damage, then releases a tornado dealing 147.5% attack damage every 0.5 seconds while expanding its range, lasting 2.5 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%, and reduces the target's healing received by 25% for 5 seconds.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%.

### Wind Field (Passive)

Each use of Tornado increases the queue's attack by 3% and reduces Tornado's cooldown by 1 second, disappearing after stacking 5 times.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Increases the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★ Increases the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★★ Boosts effect by 1%, stackable up to 7 times before disappearing.

### War Messenger (Passive)

Increase soldier attack by 4% for every 1000 soldiers deployed (up to 40% base increase). Only one War Messenger works per team; effects do not stack.

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Titan's Might** - Best for maximizing pure DPS and burst damage

- 4-piece bonus: ATK +20%

**Alternative:** Glory of the Knight - Best for skill-driven utility

**Stats Priority:**

- Attack
- Crit Rate
- Skill Amplify
- Attack Speed

## Upgrade Priority

1. **Lightning Storm (Ultimate) to ★★★** - Vital for transforming normal attacks into Chain Lightning
2. **Tornado (Slot 4) to ★★★** - Enhances damage and provides healing reduction utility
3. **Whirlwind Feathers (Slot 1) to ★★★** - Significant damage and attack speed boost
4. **Wings of Freedom (Slot 3) to ★★★** - Adds self-damage multiplier and team survivability

## Team Synergies

- **Best With**: Other Horde DPS heroes for War Messenger synergy
- **Queue Example**: Storm Maiden, Wanderer, Wilderness Hunter, Warlock, Shaman

## Tips

- Chain Lightning at ★★★ transforms Storm Maiden into a multi-target monster
- Wings of Freedom provides team-wide dodge - excellent for survival
- Wind Field stacks make Tornado increasingly powerful
- Use during AoE-heavy fights to maximize multi-target damage
