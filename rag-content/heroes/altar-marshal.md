# Altar Marshal

## Overview

- **Faction**: Nature
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Tank / Burst Damage / Control
- **Position**: Front / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Fire-Tipped Spear
- **Recommended Gear**: Fury of Blood (Primary), Glory of the Knight (Alt)
- **Gear Focus**: Max HP (Scales Ultimate and Shields)

## Skills

### Chaos Binding (Active)

**Cooldown:** 6s

Enters a protected state, gaining a shield equal to 30% of max HP that absorbs 50% of incoming damage for 6 seconds. Damage taken is reduced by 35% while active.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Shield absorption increases by 5%.
- ★★ Shield absorption increases by 5%. Activation silences 2 nearby enemies and prevents movement for 1.5s.
- ★★★ Shield absorption increases by 5%. If the shield breaks, restores 10% max HP and reduces cooldown by 1s.

### Demon-Slayer (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Transforms into Demon-Slaying form for 8 seconds. Gains 80% Max HP, 50% Attack Speed, and 35% Attack. Immune to most control effects (Stun, Knockback, Fear, Freeze, etc.).

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ HP bonus +6%. Every 2s, deals 240% Attack as True Damage to a random hero, ignoring shields.
- ★★ HP bonus +6%. Instantly casts Chaos Binding and Cosmic Ring on activation.
- ★★★ HP bonus +6%. Every 3s, hits 2 nearby enemies for 6% max HP damage and stuns for 0.5s.

### Inferno Lotus (Active)

**Cooldown:** 4s (Automatic)

Applies Burn to up to 2 enemy heroes (targets 6 if in a full Nature lineup). Deals 19.5% Attack damage/sec for 6s. Stacks up to 20 times.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Burn damage +3.5%.
- ★★ Burn damage +3.5%. Cooldown reduced by 1s.
- ★★★ Burn damage +3.5%. Each cast grants 1% Damage & 1% Damage Reduction for 6s (stacks 20x).

### Windfire Dash (Passive)

Gains 5% Block. If taking >15% max HP damage within 3s, gains 100% Dodge for 1.5s.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Block +3%.
- ★★ Block +3%.
- ★★★ Block +3%. When the dodge triggers, gains 8% max HP for 6s.

### Spirit Pearl (Passive)

Immune to Burn and Fear. All Nature heroes in the party gain +12% Damage (extra +6% when defending the castle).

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Fury of Blood** (Primary)

- Max HP scaling for Demon-Slayer and Chaos Binding shields
- Tank survivability

**Alternative:** Glory of the Knight

**Stats Priority:**

- HP (scales with ultimate and shields)
- Damage Reduction
- Block Chance

## Team Synergies

- **Nature Core**: Gains significant target scaling for Inferno Lotus and provides team-wide damage buff
- **Bonds**:
  - Monk & Petalis: 15% Damage Reduction
  - Wanderer: Celestial Bond (Hidden Synergy)

## Tips

- Demon-Slayer form provides massive stat boosts and control immunity
- Full Nature lineup increases Inferno Lotus targets from 2 to 6
- Inferno Lotus can stack 20 times - sustained burn damage
- Spirit Pearl makes Altar Marshal immune to Burn and Fear
- One of the best Nature tanks with offensive capabilities
