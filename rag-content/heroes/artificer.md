# Artificer

## Overview

- **Faction**: League
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Hybrid Support (Buff, Control)
- **Position**: Back / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Spark of Thought
- **Unlock**: Purchase or Event

## Skills

### TH-300 (Active)

Deploys a TH-300 Turret near the Artificer. It inherits 70% of Artificer's Attack, does not move or take damage, and targets Artificer's enemy. Lasts until Artificer dies.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Turret inherits an additional 20% Attack.
- ★★ Turret inherits an additional 20% Attack. Its attacks apply 1 stack of Burn (up to 3 stacks). Burn lasts 8s and deals 30% damage/sec.
- ★★★ Turret inherits an additional 20% Attack. Its attacks reduce the target's healing received by 50%.

### Upgrade! (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 24s

Upgrades 3 heroes (prioritizing others), granting them Artificer's Attack for 8s. Also upgrades the turret, increasing its Attack Speed by 50% and Attack by 100% for 8s.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Upgraded hero count +1. Increases their attack bonus by 2.5%. Upgraded heroes deal 40% more damage to shields.
- ★★ Upgraded hero count +1. Increases attack bonus by 2.5%. Upgraded heroes also deal additional damage equal to 250% of Artificer's Attack with each attack.
- ★★★ Upgraded hero count +1. When a hero is upgraded, their current skill cooldown is reduced by 15%.

### Pulse Mod (Active)

Improves TH-300 to fire a Pulse Bullet every 6 normal attacks, dealing damage equal to 200% of Attack to all enemies in its path.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage +30%. Pulse Bullet stuns targets for 0.5 seconds.
- ★★ Skill damage +30%.
- ★★★ Skill damage +30%. Pulse Bullet trigger requirement reduced by 1 attack.

### Parts Collection (Passive)

When nearby heroes (excluding herself) use skills, Artificer collects parts. Every 18 parts strengthens the next normal attack to deal 135% damage to all enemies in range.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage +15%. Every part collected restores 1% of max HP to all heroes.
- ★★ Skill damage +15%.
- ★★★ Skill damage +15%. Strengthened normal attacks stun the target for 1 second.

### Magnifier (Active)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Empowers 1 hero (prioritizing others), increasing their Max HP by 7.5% and damage by 10% for 8s.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Empowered hero count +1.
- ★★ Empowered hero count +1. During the effect, recover 3% of max HP per second.
- ★★★ Empowered hero count +1. During effect, target can resist control effects once.

### Flickering Watch (Passive)

In PvP (battles against other Lords), all League heroes start with 20% reduced initial ultimate cooldown and 10% increased skill damage.

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Glory of the Knight** (Default)

- Stats: +10% Attack, +20% HP, +4% Skill Amp
- Ability damage + some durability

**Alternative:** Fury of Blood

**Stats Priority:**

- Attack (turret inherits stats)
- Skill Amplify
- HP

## Team Synergies

- **League Core**: Flickering Watch benefits all League heroes in PvP
- **Best With**: High DPS heroes who benefit from Upgrade! buffs

## Tips

- TH-300 turret is immortal - it only dies when Artificer dies
- ★★★ turret reduces enemy healing by 50% - counters healers
- Upgrade! at ★★★ reduces ally skill cooldowns by 15%
- Parts Collection provides team-wide healing and stuns
- Flickering Watch is a hidden OP passive for League PvP teams
