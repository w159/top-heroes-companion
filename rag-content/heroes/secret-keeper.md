When fighting against players, grant allies a shield equal to [x]% of the HP. This shield lasts for 6 seconds. Each time damage is taken, 40% of it can be absorbed by the shield.

⭐ Boosts shield absorption by 2.5%. 8 seconds after the battle stars, grants all allied heroes a shield equal to 4% of the Secret Keeper's max HP every 8 seconds. The shield absorbs 40% of incoming damage and lasts for 4 seconds. (Requires 5 yellow stars)

⭐⭐ Shield absorption increases by 2.5%. (Requires 5 red stars)

⭐⭐⭐ Boost shield absorption by 2.5%, and increases the shield value gained by allied League Heroes by 30%. The absorption of periodic shields is increased to 10%. (Requires 3 silver stars)
