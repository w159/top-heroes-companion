# Tidecaller

## Overview

- **Faction**: Nature
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Support / Summoner / Healer
- **Specialties**: Damage Dealer, Control, Summon
- **Position**: Back / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Divine Trident
- **Recommended Gear**: Fury of Blood (Primary), Glory of the Knight (Alt)

## Skills

### Hydro Hammer (Active)

**Cooldown:** 10s

Strikes targets and nearby enemies with a water-formed hammer, dealing 245% of Attack as damage, stunning them for 1 second, and summoning a Guardian Water Elemental that inherits 80% of Tidecaller's Attack and HP for 12 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Guardian Water Elemental explodes upon death, dealing damage equal to 10% of its HP to nearby targets.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 25%. Guardian Water Elemental's inherited attributes increase by 10%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 25%. Guardian Water Elemental's inherited attributes increase by 10%, and an additional Guardian Water Elemental is summoned.

### Ascend (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 21s

Unleashes a giant water pillar that deals 365% of Attack as damage, knocking enemies into the air and summoning a Water Elemental Lord that inherits 157.5% of Tidecaller's Attack and HP. The Lord has no time limit, is immune to control, and only one can be active.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Water Elemental Lord explodes upon death, dealing damage equal to 10% of its HP to a wide range of targets.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 35%. Water Elemental Lord's inherited attributes increase by 12.5% and it gains 25% damage reduction.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 35%. Water Elemental Lord can summon 2 small water elementals that inherit 40% of its attributes and last 10 seconds (Cooldown: 5s).

### Surge (Passive)

Each of Tidecaller's normal attacks heals all team members, restoring HP equal to 152.5% of Attack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ HP recovery increases by 17.5%.
- ★★ HP recovery increases by 17.5%.
- ★★★ Healing is increased by 17.5%. For every 1 of your units that die or are sacrificed (including summoned units), Ascend's cooldown is reduced by 0.5 seconds.

### Evolve (Passive)

Tidecaller's normal attacks can now hit an additional target, dealing extra damage equal to 57.5% of Attack.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Additional damage increases by 7.5%. Guardian Water Elemental can perform a heavy strike that stuns for 1 second (Cooldown: 8s).
- ★★ Additional damage increases by 7.5%. Ranged Water Elemental's normal attacks now pierce enemies and apply 3% vulnerability for 6 seconds, stacking up to 5 times.
- ★★★ Additional damage increases by 7.5%. Water Elemental Lord's attack speed increases by 60%.

### Purification Lance (Active)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Fires a water lance that pierces enemies, dealing 367.5% of Attack as damage and summoning a Ranged Water Elemental that inherits 57.5% of Tidecaller's Attack and HP for 8 seconds. The lance randomly dispels 1 buff from hit targets.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage increases by 42.5%. Ranged Water Elemental's inherited attributes increase by 7.5%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 42.5%. Ranged Water Elemental's inherited attributes increase by 7.5%, and it fires an additional lance (without dispel).
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 42.5%. Ranged Water Elemental's inherited attributes increase by 7.5%.

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Fury of Blood** (Primary)

- Focuses on HP/Sustain for summons/healing
- Summons scale with Tidecaller's stats

**Alternative:** Glory of the Knight

**Stats Priority:**

- HP (summons inherit stats)
- Attack (healing and damage scales)
- Skill Amplify

## Team Synergies

- **Bonds**: Pixie & Monk (15% Damage Reduction)
- **Best With**: Nature teams for faction synergy
- **Skins**: Ice Queen, Mint Ice, Ocean Princess, Pastry Pro

## Tips

- Water Elemental Lord at ★★★ spawns additional elementals - massive battlefield control
- Surge provides team-wide healing with every normal attack
- Summons inherit your stats - maximize HP and Attack
- Purification Lance dispels enemy buffs - timing is key
- One of the best Nature summoners and healers combined
