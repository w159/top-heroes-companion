# Wanderer

## Overview

- **Faction**: Horde
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Tank / DPS / Control
- **Position**: Back / Mid
- **Unique Weapon**: Fire-Tipped Spear

## Skills

### Immobilize (Active)

**Cooldown:** 6s

Deals damage equal to 405% of Attack to a single target, immobilizing it for 1.5 seconds. Instantly kills summoned units.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Over 40% damage increase. Target receives 25% additional damage from Wanderer for 3s.
- ★★ Damage increase by 40%.
- ★★★ Damage increase by 40%. Immobilization extended by 1s.

### Illusory Split (Ultimate)

**Cooldown:** 15s

Summons a giant duplicate behind 3 random enemies. Duplicates inherit 90% of Wanderer's Attack and HP, last 9s, and take 95% less damage.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Duplicate attributes increased by 10%. When a duplicate vanishes, Wanderer gains a shield (10% max HP, 40% damage absorption, stacks 3x).
- ★★ Duplicate attributes increased by 10%. Each active duplicate boosts Wanderer's damage by 8% and reduces damage taken by 8%.
- ★★★ Duplicate attributes increased by 10%. Duplicates can perform Staff Stances and gain effects from True Sight and Robust Constitution.

### Robust Constitution (Passive)

Blocks the next instance of damage every 6s. Successful blocks reduce skill damage taken by 65% for 2s.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Increases Crit Resistance by 20% after blocking.
- ★★ Block interval reduced by 1.5s.
- ★★★ Gains resistance to stun effects.

### Staff Stances (Passive)

Normal attacks deal extra damage equal to 150% of Attack, with a 50% chance to sweep in a cone.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Normal attack damage +35%. Normal attacks boost Attack Speed by 15% (stacks 3x).
- ★★ Normal attack damage +35%. Sweep chance becomes 100%.
- ★★★ Normal attack damage +35%. 40% chance for a smash strike (frontal rectangle), +50% damage and +30% Crit Rate.

### True Sight (Passive)

Shield-break power +200%. Damage to summoned units +250%.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Ignores 25% of target's Block.
- ★★ Ignores 25% of target's Crit Resistance.
- ★★★ Ignores 20% of target's Damage Reduction.

### The Great Sage (Passive)

Team-wide buff: 1.5% HP Drain, 2% Attack Speed, and 1.5% Attack. Effects double for each Horde hero in the team (Max: 9% HP Drain, 12% AS, 9% Atk).

## Recommended Build

### Gear Set

**Titan's Might** (4-Piece)

Clones deal the most damage; Titan's Might maximizes their output.

- 4-Piece Bonus: +20% Attack, +3% Damage Increase

**Alternative:** Glory of the Knight

- Bonus: +10% Attack, +20% HP, +4% Skill Amplify

**Stats Priority:**

- Attack (primary)
- Max HP (for clone scaling)
- Crit Rate
- Damage Increase

## Team Synergies

- **Celestial Bond**: Forms a hidden bond with Altar Marshal
- **Best With**: Horde heroes to maximize The Great Sage buff
- **Queue Example**: Wanderer, Barbarian, Wilderness Hunter, Soulmancer, Shaman, Warlock

## Awakening

- **Tier**: S-Tier Mythic
- **Priority**: High - One of the strongest Horde carries
- **Soul Stones Required**: 9 total

## Tips

- Duplicates inherit your stats - maximize Attack for clone damage
- The Great Sage passive makes Wanderer essential in Horde teams
- True Sight counters shield-heavy teams and summoner heroes
- Robust Constitution provides excellent survivability against burst damage
