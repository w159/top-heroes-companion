## Skills

### Commanding Call[]
Description:Summon a wave of arrows, dealing [x]% damage to enemies in a rectangular area in the front. Paragon and allies gain a shield equal to [x]% of Attack for 5 seconds. Shield absorbs 40% of damage taken. Cooldown: 10 seconds.

- ★  Increases shield absorption by 15%, skill damage by 40%, and the area of skill damage by 25%.
- ★★ Shield absorption value increased by 15%, skill damage increased by 40%.
- ★★★ Increases shield absorption by 15%, skill damage by 40%. After casting, the Paragon gains 20% skill damage reduction for 5 seconds.

### Celestial Blade[]
Description:Ultimate Skill: Paragon wields a sacred sword, gaining crowd control immunity for 1 second. The sword strikes all enemies in a large area 3 times, dealing a total of [x]% damage and applying 3 Burn stacks. Grants the Paragon 50% damage reduction for 1 second. Cooldown: 21 seconds.
Burn: Deals 30% damage per second for 8 seconds.

- ★ Skill damage additionally increases by 65%. Immunity to control effects and the duration of damage reduction effects increase by 1 second.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 65%.
- ★★★ Skill damage additionally increases by 65%. When Celestial Blade defeats a target, its cooldown is reduced by 2 seconds.

### Final Verdict[]
Description:All attacks by the Paragon against enemies with HP higher than 70% cause an increase in damage by [x]%.

- ★ Increase damage dealt by 5%.
- ★★ Increase damage dealt by 5%.
- ★★★ Damage bonus additionally increases by 5%. When there are 4 or more League heroes in the lineup, Celestial Blade's damage increases by 35%.

### Moral Fury[]
Description:Banner of Unity: Raises all allies' Attack by [x]% and deals [x]% damage per second to the nearest 3 enemies for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 15 seconds.

- ★ Increases skill damage bonus by 18.75%, and buff effect by 2.5%.
- ★★ Increases skill damage bonus by 18.75%, and buff effect by 2.5%.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 56.25%, bonus effect increases by 7.5%. After casting, grants all your units a skill shield equal to 240% of Paragon's Attack, which absorbs 100% of skill damage for 8 seconds.

### Ultimate Execution[]
Description:Enchant the longsword with flames, extending Paragon's normal attack range and allowing an additional attack on another target. Increase Paragon's normal attack damage by [x]%.

- ★ Normal attack damage bonus increases by 2.5%.
- ★★ Normal attack damage bonus increases by 2.5%.
- ★★★ Normal attack damage bonus increases by 2.5%.

### Conqueror[]
Description:When deployed, each legendary or higher-tier League hero in the team increases castle attack effectiveness by 12%. If the lineup has 6 league heroes, it grants an additional 10% skill critical rate to the whole team.
