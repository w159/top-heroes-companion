# Warlock

## Lore

A former scholar of forbidden arts, Warlock sacrificed his soul in exchange for limitless magical power. His twisted chants and dark incantations channel shadowflame and chaos, tearing through enemy ranks with merciless precision. Few dare face him without warding spells or protection from the arcane.
