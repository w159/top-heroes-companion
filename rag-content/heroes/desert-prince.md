# Desert Prince

## Overview

- **Faction**: Horde
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Role**: Tank (Multi-Target, Sustained)
- **Position**: Mid / Back
- **Unlock**: Purchase or Event

## Skills

### Royal Armor (Active)

**Cooldown:** 10s

Gains a shield equal to 550% of Attack, absorbing 50% of incoming damage and lasting 8 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Shield absorption increases by 50%. When the shield breaks, Desert Prince gains 35% damage reduction for 6 seconds.
- ★★ Shield absorption increases by 50%.
- ★★★ Shield absorption increases by 50%. While the shield is active, Desert Prince reflects 40% of damage taken back to the attacker.

### Scorching Scimitar (Active)

**Cooldown:** 6s

Swings dual blades to inflict true damage equal to 22% of max HP on nearby enemies (capped at 1000% of Attack).

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Max HP increases by 2%. Stun duration on targets is extended by 1.5 seconds.
- ★★ Max HP increases by 2%.
- ★★★ Max HP increases by 2%. Desert Prince's Attack Speed increases by 50% for 6 seconds after using Scorching Scimitar.

### Royal Treasury (Passive)

Royal Armor enhances all allies, increasing their skill damage by 15% and reducing skill damage taken by 22.5% for 6 seconds.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Skill damage reduction effect increases by 2.5%.
- ★★ Skill damage reduction effect increases by 2.5%.
- ★★★ Skill damage reduction effect increases by 2.5%. The effect also triggers immediately when Desert Prince is defeated.

### Eye of Insight (Passive)

Reduces skill damage taken by Desert Prince by 32.5%.

**Star Upgrades:**

- ★ Effect bonus increased by 2.5%.
- ★★ Effect bonus increased by 7.5%.
- ★★★ When HP falls below 30%, Desert Prince gains skill damage immunity for 2 seconds (30s Cooldown).

### Enthusiast (Passive)

When deployed, each Legendary or higher rarity Horde hero in the Queue increases the enemy's death and wounded ratio by 5% in battle.

- **Horde Core**: Enthusiast benefits Horde teams
- **Best With**: Other Horde tanks and DPS

## Tips

- True damage from Scorching Scimitar ignores armor - scales with HP
- ★★★ Royal Armor reflects 40% damage back to attackers
- Royal Treasury buffs entire team while Royal Armor is active
- Eye of Insight at ★★★ grants skill immunity at low HP - clutch survival
- Enthusiast is a hidden strong passive for Horde-focused teams
- One of the tankiest Horde heroes with offensive utility
