# Epic Hero Passive Traits

Once Epic Heroes reach 2-Star Platinum, they unlock a global passive trait (Skill 3). Prioritize maxing these out!

| Hero | Passive Bonus (Max Lv 30) | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brawler | +30% Tech Research Speed | HIGHEST |
| Knight | +30% Training Speed | HIGHEST |
| Ranger | +30% Ruby Gathering Speed | HIGH |
| Minister | +30% Soldier Healing Speed | MEDIUM |
| Outlaw | +30% Wood Gathering Speed | LOW |
