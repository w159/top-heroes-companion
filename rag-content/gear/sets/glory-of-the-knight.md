# Glory of the Knight

**Type**: Offensive Set
**Role**: DPS / Caster / Carry

## Overview

"Glory of the Knight" is the standard offensive set. While it provides a healthy HP boost, its main feature is the "Skill Damage Amplify," which is critical for ability-based heroes.

## Set Bonuses

### 2-Piece Bonus

**Gear Attack +40%**

- Adds raw killing power.

**Gear HP +80%**

- Provides baseline survivability (half of the Fury set's HP).

### 4-Piece Bonus

**Skill Damage Amplify +8%**

- This is the money stat. It multiplies the damage of Ultimates and Active Skills.

## Recommended Heroes

### League Faction (High Priority)

League heroes are often "Casters" who rely on big ultimate nukes.

- **Paragon**: Her entire value is her ultimate damage.
- **Rose Princess**: Needs the skill amp for her control/damage mix.

### General DPS

- **Storm Maiden** (Horde)
- **Forest Maiden** (Nature DPS variant)
