# Fury of Blood

**Type**: Defensive Set
**Role**: Tank / Healer / Sustain

## Overview

"Fury of Blood" is the standard defensive set. It provides massive raw HP and damage reduction, making it the only choice for Frontline tanks and HP-scaling healers.

## Set Bonuses

### 2-Piece Bonus

**Gear HP +160%**

- This is a massive multiplier to the _gear's_ base HP stat.

### 4-Piece Bonus

**Damage Reduction +6%**

- A rare "Final Damage Reduction" multiplier that scales extremely well in late-game PvP.

## Recommended Heroes

### Nature Faction (High Priority)

Nature heroes love this set because their entire kit often scales with Max HP.

- **Sage**: Heals scale with HP.
- **Treant**: Shields/Tankiness scale with HP.
- **Tidecaller**: Summons inherit HP.

### General Tanks

- **Adjudicator** (League)
- **Beastmaster** (Horde)
- **Secret Keeper**
