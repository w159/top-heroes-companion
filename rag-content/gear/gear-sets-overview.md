# Gear Sets Guide

Top Heroes has three primary gear sets, each optimized for different hero roles and damage scaling.

## Gear Set Overview

| Set                     | Attack Bonus | HP Bonus | Special              |
| ----------------------- | ------------ | -------- | -------------------- |
| **Glory of the Knight** | +40%         | +80%     | Skill Damage +8%     |
| **Fury of Blood**       | -            | +160%    | Damage Reduction +6% |
| **Titan's Might**       | +80%         | -        | Damage Increase +6%  |

## When to Use Each Set

### Glory of the Knight (Knight Set)

**Best for**: Skill-based damage dealers

Heroes whose damage comes from skill multipliers rather than flat attack:

- Pyromancer (League)
- Paragon (League)
- Bishop (League)
- Pixie (Nature)
- Tidecaller (Nature - alt build)

### Fury of Blood (Blood Set)

**Best for**: Tanks, healers, and Nature faction

Essential for survivability-focused heroes:

- **All tanks**: Adjudicator, Steelward, Treeguard, etc.
- **All healers**: Nun, Forest Maiden, Witch
- **Entire Nature faction** (sustain-focused meta)
- Support heroes needing to stay alive

### Titan's Might (Titan Set)

**Best for**: Flat attack scalers and raw DPS

Heroes with direct attack damage scaling:

- Wanderer (Horde) - primary carry
- Storm Maiden (Horde)
- Wukong (Horde)
- Desert Prince (damage build)
- Astrologer (League)

| **Boots**  | Attack       | Critical Attack     |
| **Helmet** | HP           | Block Chance        |
| **Chest**  | HP           | Block Chance        |

## Upgrade Priority

### For DPS/Healers

1. Sword (max attack scaling)
2. Boots (secondary attack)
3. Helmet/Chest (survival)

### For Tanks

1. Helmet (max HP)
2. Chest (secondary HP)
3. Sword/Boots (minor damage)

## Enhancement Strategy

### Level in Increments of 10

- Extra buffs unlock at Lv 10, 20, 30, 40
- Level all 4 pieces to same level for **Enhancement Master** buff (+Attack/HP)

### Titan Marks

- Add faction-specific +30% bonuses
- Obtainable from Guild Vault and Star Shop
- Always use on your main faction's heroes

### Promotion

- Unlocks at Lv 40
- Requires Legendary/Mythic Promotion Stones
