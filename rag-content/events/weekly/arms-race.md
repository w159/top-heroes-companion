# Arms Race (Weekly Event)

**Frequency**: Weekly (Monday - Saturday)
**Type**: Server Development & Economy

## Overview

The Arms Race is the primary weekly rhythm of Top Heroes. Every day focuses on a specific aspect of growth.
**Golden Rule**: Do not use speedups, shards, or recruitment tokens _unless_ it is the specific day for them during Arms Race.

## Schedule

### Monday: Territory Building

**Focus**: Construction & Power

- **Tasks**:
  - Use Construction Speedups.
  - Spend Timber/Stone/Rubies.
  - Boost Facility Power.
  - Consume Relic Shards (sometimes linked).
- **Strategy**: Start long upgrades (like Castle) days in advance so they _finish_ on Monday morning.

### Tuesday: Tech Boost

**Focus**: Research

- **Tasks**:
  - Use Research Speedups.
  - Boost Tech Power.
- **Strategy**: Save your longest researches to complete today.

### Wednesday: Global Battles

**Focus**: Stamina & Gathering

- **Tasks**:
  - Kill Mobs (Lv 1-30).
  - Kill Bosses.
  - Gather Resources (Rubies give key points).
- **Strategy**: Save "Bounty Quests" (up to 40) to turn in today.

### Thursday: Hero Development

**Focus**: Training & EXP

- **Tasks**:
  - Train Troops.
  - Use EXP books.

### Friday: Equipment & Forging

**Focus**: Gear

- **Tasks**:
  - Enhance Gear.
  - Promote Gear.

### Saturday/Sunday: Sprint

**Focus**: All of the above (often combined).

## Rewards

- **Diamonds**
- **Universal Speedups**
- **Relic Shards**
